Stock Status Dashboard

Visually identify stock levels on your shelves.

Use these buttons to light up LEDs for all tracked parts. The color indicates the part's total stock level:

  • ● Green: Stock is OK (above reorder point).
  • ● Yellow: Stock is low (at or below reorder point).
  • ● Red: Stock is critical (at or below minimum).
